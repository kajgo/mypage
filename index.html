I'm a computer engineer with great interest in the art of programming and the craft of software development. Working in the area does not always satisfy my coding appetite so I also spend lots of my spare time programming. Follow the github link in the upper right corner to see some of my recent hobby hacks or check out Photobox, an android project I'm working on together with my friend and colleague Rickard Lindberg.

Besides computer related stuff I spend a lot of my time rock climbing and consider a day at some crag working on a project or bouldering on a slightly too big stone a good day. More climbing related content (mostly in Swedish) can be found at "Bergstrollet".

From time to another I travel to different places, both business and pleasure. Visiting other cultures always provides new experiences and insights and I've collected some of them in the "blog meep, meep... wrooom!" which is written from a Swedish point of view and in Swedish.
